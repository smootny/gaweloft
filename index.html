ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Kacper Gawel, an interior designer with a passion for transforming spaces into modern masterpieces that not only reflect contemporary elegance but also enhance functionality. Having graduated from the prestigious Faculty of Architecture at Wroclaw University of Science and Technology, I've spent the past five years perfecting the art of interior and garden design.


My design philosophy centers around creating modern lofts that embody style and practicality, yet I'm always eager to explore new challenges and styles in a variety of spaces. Whether you're looking to rejuvenate your residential environment or revitalize your commercial space, I approach every project with a fresh perspective and a commitment to bringing your vision to life.
